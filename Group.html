THE1975

The 1975 is an English pop rock band formed in 2002 in Wilmslow, Cheshire. The band consists of lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist Matthew Healy, lead guitarist Adam Hann, bassist Ross MacDonald, and drummer George Daniel. They gained widespread recognition for their distinctive sound that blends various genres, including pop, rock, and electronic elements.

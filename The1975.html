About Us

The 1975

Introduction: The 1975 is a British pop rock band formed in 2002 in Wilmslow, Cheshire. The band consists of four members: Matthew Healy (lead vocals, rhythm guitar), Adam Hann (lead guitar, backing vocals), Ross MacDonald (bass guitar, keyboards, backing vocals), and George Daniel (drums, backing vocals, keyboards, producer). The band is known for its eclectic and genre-blending sound, incorporating elements of pop, rock, electronic, and R&B. Formation and Early Years: The band's origins trace back to their high school years, with the original lineup coming together while the members were still teenagers. They experimented with various musical styles before officially forming as The 1975. The early years were marked by a gradual evolution of their sound.
